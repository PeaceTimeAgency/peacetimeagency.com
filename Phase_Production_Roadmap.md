# Peace Time Agency: Production Phase Roadmap

Welcome to the Peace Time Agency project roadmap. This document outlines the progression of our digital platform from initial development through to full-scale operations. 

We are currently in **Phase 1: Pre-Launch**, focusing on building a robust, high-fidelity foundation for the agency's online presence.

---

## 🟢 PHASE 1: Pre-Launch (Current Phase)
**Goal:** Establish a premium digital footprint, secure technical infrastructure, and prepare the core systems for creator onboarding.

### 1. Brand & Aesthetic Finalization
*   **Design System:** Implementation of the "Midnight-on-Rails" high-end, cyberpunk-inspired luxury aesthetic.
*   **UI/UX:** Development of a "Triple-A" user interface featuring smooth Framer Motion animations, glassmorphism, and responsive layouts.

### 2. Core Platform Development
*   **Front-Page Dashboard:** Dynamic hero section, "Who's Live Now" showcase, and interactive service value propositions.
*   **Creator Roster (`/creators`):** A dedicated, cinematic showcase for the agency's elite talent.
*   **Agency News (`/news`):** A central hub for updates, announcements, and press.

### 3. Recruitment Infrastructure
*   **Application Portal (`/apply`):** A seamless, multi-step application form optimized for conversion.
*   **Backend Integration:** Real-time data routing connecting the application form directly to the agency's secure Discord channels via webhooks for immediate team notification.

### 4. Technical Setup & Pre-Deployment
*   **Framework:** Built on the modern Next.js 15 React framework for maximum performance and SEO.
*   **Deployment:** Staging environments set up via Vercel for rapid iteration and testing.

---

## 🟡 PHASE 2: Launch & Acquisition
**Goal:** Officially launch the platform to the public and begin active talent recruitment and community building.

*   **Public Release:** Pushing the finalized website to the primary production domain.
*   **Recruitment Drive:** Driving traffic to the Application Portal to onboard the first wave of elite TikTok LIVE creators.
*   **Community Gateway:** Opening the Discord community to accepted applications and establishing the operational communication flow.
*   **Data & Analytics Tracking:** Monitoring website traffic, application conversion rates, and user engagement to refine the UI.

---

## 🔵 PHASE 3: Operations & Scaling
**Goal:** Deepen platform functionality, integrate advanced API features, and scale agency operations.

*   **Advanced TikTok Integrations:** Connecting directly to TikTok LIVE APIs (where applicable) to automate "Who's Live Now" data and creator statistics.
*   **Creator Dashboards (Future Scope):** Potential development of internalized hubs for talent to track their growth, earnings, and schedules.
*   **Continuous Optimization:** Regular A/B testing of the landing page, refining the aesthetic, and expanding the agency's digital footprint based on user feedback.

---
